About Us

Who We Are

Lumeva, a veteran-owned, mission-driven organization founded in 2024, delivers innovative solutions to federal, state, and local government agencies. With expertise in Systems Engineering and Technical Assistance (SETA), cybersecurity, program management, and policy analysis, Lumeva addresses mission-critical challenges with precision and excellence.

Backed by advanced certifications and a team holding Top Secret/SCI clearance, we ensure secure, efficient execution of sensitive projects. We are small enough to provide direct access to management for employees and clients, yet large enough to fulfill our commitments on the most demanding engagements.

Explore Our Services

Mission

To empower government and defense agencies with innovative, secure, and efficient solutions that drive mission success while fostering trust, collaboration, and sustainable development.

Vision

To be the leading provider of tailored technical and strategic services for government and defense sectors, setting a benchmark for excellence, innovation, and integrity in the industry.

Our Services

What We Do

01
Artificial Intelligence

Lumeva harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to transform how government and defense agencies operate. From predictive analytics to automated decision-support systems, we design and deploy AI solutions that enhance mission effectiveness and reduce operational risk.

02
Cloud

Lumeva delivers secure, scalable cloud solutions tailored to the unique demands of government and defense environments. We architect, migrate, and manage cloud infrastructure across AWS, Azure, and hybrid environments while maintaining strict compliance with FedRAMP, FISMA, and DoD security requirements.

03
Data Solutions

Lumeva transforms raw data into actionable intelligence through advanced analytics, visualization, and data governance frameworks. Using tools such as SQL and Tableau, we help agencies consolidate disparate data sources and improve decision-making.

04
Cyber Operations

Lumeva provides end-to-end cybersecurity services designed to protect mission-critical systems and sensitive information from evolving threats, including Zero Trust architecture, RMF implementation, threat hunting, incident response, and continuous monitoring.

05
System Engineering and Technical Assistance

Lumeva's SETA services provide government clients with the technical expertise and program support needed to optimize complex systems and achieve mission success, from requirements definition through testing, integration, and sustainment.

06
National Security

Lumeva supports the nation's most critical security missions with strategic analysis, intelligence support, and technical solutions, drawing on direct experience with INSCOM, SOCOM, NGA, NSA, and the FBI.

07
Professional Services

Lumeva's professional services practice delivers the program management, policy analysis, and strategic consulting expertise that government agencies need to navigate complex challenges, from managing multimillion-dollar portfolios to crafting regulatory frameworks and organizational transformation strategies.
