Dr. Xavier Stewart

Brigadier General, U.S. Army (Ret.) - Strategic Advisor

Dr. Xavier Stewart is a distinguished leader in homeland defense, national security, and public health, with a proven track record in combating weapons of mass destruction (CWMD). A retired Brigadier General in the U.S. Army, his career reflects an unwavering commitment to advancing defense strategies, intelligence operations, and emergency preparedness at the highest levels.

As Principal of Stewart and Associates, Dr. Stewart provides strategic guidance to the Department of Defense (DoD) and the Intelligence Community, specializing in counter-WMD strategies, risk mitigation, and crisis response. Holding a doctorate in Public Health, Dr. Stewart is a Board-Certified Forensic Examiner, Respiratory Care Practitioner, and Nationally Registered EMT. Our Services

What We Do

Artificial Intelligence

Lumeva harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to transform how government and defense agencies operate. From predictive analytics to automated decision-support systems, we design and deploy AI solutions that enhance mission effectiveness and reduce operational risk. Our team integrates AI capabilities into existing frameworks, ensuring secure, scalable, and responsible adoption aligned with federal standards.

Cloud

Lumeva delivers secure, scalable cloud solutions tailored to the unique demands of government and defense environments. We architect, migrate, and manage cloud infrastructure across AWS, Azure, and hybrid environments while maintaining strict compliance with FedRAMP, FISMA, and DoD security requirements. Our cloud engineering expertise ensures agencies achieve operational agility without compromising security posture.

Data Solutions

Lumeva transforms raw data into actionable intelligence through advanced analytics, visualization, and data governance frameworks. Using tools such as SQL and Tableau, we help agencies consolidate disparate data sources, improve decision-making, and drive mission outcomes. Our data solutions are built with security and integrity at the core, ensuring classified and sensitive data is handled with the highest standards.

Cyber Operations

Lumeva provides end-to-end cybersecurity services designed to protect mission-critical systems and sensitive information from evolving threats. Our capabilities span Zero Trust architecture, RMF implementation, threat hunting, incident response, and continuous monitoring. With a team holding active TS/SCI clearances, we deliver cyber operations that meet the most demanding national security requirements.

System Engineering and Assistance

Lumeva's SETA services provide government clients with the technical expertise and program support needed to optimize complex systems and achieve mission success. We embed experienced engineers and analysts directly into programs, offering lifecycle support from requirements definition through testing, integration, and sustainment. Our SETA professionals bring hands-on experience from defense and intelligence programs, delivering results with military precision.

National Security

Lumeva supports the nation's most critical security missions by providing strategic analysis, intelligence support, and technical solutions across defense and intelligence community programs. Our team draws on direct experience with organizations including INSCOM, SOCOM, NGA, NSA, and the FBI to deliver capabilities that address emerging threats and strengthen national resilience. We operate at the intersection of policy, technology, and operations to ensure our clients stay ahead of adversaries.

Professional Services

Lumeva's professional services practice delivers the program management, policy analysis, and strategic consulting expertise that government agencies need to navigate complex challenges. From managing multimillion-dollar portfolios to crafting regulatory frameworks and organizational transformation strategies, our consultants bring discipline and results-orientation to every engagement. We serve as trusted advisors to senior leadership, translating complex problems into clear, executable solutions.
