Life at Lumeva

Who We Are as a Team

At Lumeva, we believe our people are our most powerful asset. As a veteran-owned, mission-driven organization, we're committed to pushing boundaries, championing innovation, and creating secure, efficient solutions for government and defense agencies.

Flexibility and Well-Being

Our employees and their well-being come first, which is why we offer flexible work options and competitive leave programs. Our Live Well. Be Well. Wellness program offers activities that translate into monthly wellness credits and quarterly prize drawings.

Culture

Integrity & Accountability

Guided by strong ethics and a commitment to excellence, we hold ourselves to the highest standards, ensuring trust and reliability with every solution we deliver.

Innovation & Excellence

We leverage the latest technologies and best practices to deliver solutions that exceed expectations, constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Mission-Driven

Every project we undertake is driven by a commitment to mission success. We understand the critical nature of the work our clients perform.

Points of Pride

01

Veteran-owned and operated, bringing military discipline, strategic thinking, and a service-first ethos to every engagement with government and defense agencies.

02

Our team holds Top Secret/SCI clearances and advanced certifications including PMP, CISM, AWS Solutions Architect, and CFCM.

03

We embrace cutting-edge technologies such as advanced data analytics and cloud computing to deliver forward-looking solutions.

Lumeva Gives Back

Since the beginning, we've been committed to uplifting underdeveloped communities. We proudly donate 5% of our annual profit to the Helping Light Foundation, supporting orphaned and struggling children in Togo.

Closer to home, we partner with Kingdom Youth Development, a Tampa Bay nonprofit that equips young people with academic tutoring, mentorship, and leadership development to help them thrive as community leaders.
